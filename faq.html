FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is toolnova.tools really free?

Yes — every tool is completely free, with no usage limits, no watermarks added to your output, and no premium tier. Nothing to upgrade to.

Do you store my files?

No. Most of our tools run entirely inside your browser using JavaScript, so your files are processed on your own device and never get uploaded to our servers.

Do I need to create an account?

No. You can use any tool immediately — no signup, no email, no login.

Is it safe to use toolnova.tools for sensitive documents?

Since processing happens locally in your browser rather than on a remote server, your files aren't transmitted over the internet in most cases — which is generally safer than online tools that upload your files elsewhere.

Why do I see ads on the site?

Ads (served through Google AdSense) help cover hosting and development costs, which is what lets us keep every tool free with no paywalls. See our Privacy Policy for details on how ad data is handled.

Can I use toolnova.tools on my phone?

Yes, the entire site and all tools are designed to work on mobile browsers as well as desktop.

I found a bug or have a tool suggestion — who do I tell?

We'd love to hear it — please contact us and pick "Suggestion" or "Complaint" as the reason.

Which browsers are supported?

Current versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge are all supported. For the best experience, keep your browser up to date.
