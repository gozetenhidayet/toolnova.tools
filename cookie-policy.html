Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This page explains what cookies toolnova.tools uses, why, and how you can control them. It works alongside our Privacy Policy.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They help websites remember information about your visit, like preferences or, in the case of ads, what you may be interested in.

Cookies we use

CategoryPurposeSet by
AnalyticsUnderstand how visitors use the site (pages viewed, time on site)Google Analytics
AdvertisingShow and measure ads, sometimes based on prior browsingGoogle AdSense
PreferencesRemember your chosen display languageGoogle Translate widget

We do not use cookies to track the content of files you process with our tools — those never leave your browser.

Managing cookies

  • Most browsers let you block or delete cookies in their settings menu.
  • You can opt out of personalized Google ads at adssettings.google.com.
  • You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking with the Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on.

Blocking cookies may affect some features, like remembering your language choice, but all tools will still work.

Questions?

Contact us if you have any questions about our use of cookies.
