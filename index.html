Are Toolnova tools free?

Yes. The tools on this page can be used without a paid account. The site may display advertising to help support free access.

Are my uploaded files stored?

Toolnova's file tools are designed to process files in your browser. The page does not intentionally upload your selected documents to a Toolnova server. Third-party libraries may be downloaded to your browser so the tools can run.

Do the calculators provide professional advice?

No. Health and financial calculator results are estimates for general information. Verify important decisions with a qualified professional or official source.

Why can a converted document look different?

Complex layouts, uncommon fonts, scanned pages and protected files can affect conversion quality. Review downloaded files before relying on them.

How can I report a problem?

Use the Contact link in the footer and include the tool name, browser, device and a description of what happened. Do not send sensitive documents.

Important: Tool results are provided for general informational and utility purposes. Always review converted files and independently verify medical, financial, legal or safety-related results.
