What we do

toolnova.tools is a collection of over 40 browser-based tools spanning PDF conversion, OCR, calculators, generators (QR codes, passwords, and more), and file converters. Every tool is free, with no usage limits, no watermarks on your output, and no account required.

How it works

Most of our tools run entirely inside your browser using JavaScript — your files are processed on your own device and are never uploaded to our servers. That means faster results, and it means we never see, store, or have access to your documents, images, or data.

How we stay free

We show a small number of ads (via Google AdSense) to cover hosting costs. We don't sell your data, and we never will — see our Privacy Policy for the full details on how ads and analytics work on this site.

Our commitment

  • Tools stay free — no surprise paywalls
  • No signup required to use any tool
  • Your files are processed locally, not stored on our servers
  • Clean, ad-labeled experience — we don't hide ads as content
