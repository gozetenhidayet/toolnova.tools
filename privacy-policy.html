Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

1. Overview

toolnova.tools and the toolnova.tools Chrome Extension are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect (if any) and how we use it.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All tools on toolnova.tools run entirely in your browser. No data you enter into our tools (text, numbers, URLs, etc.) is sent to our servers.

3. Chrome Extension

The toolnova.tools Chrome Extension does not collect any user data. The extension uses the following permissions:

activeTab: Used solely to read the current page URL when you choose to generate a QR code for the current page. This data is not stored or transmitted.

contextMenus: Used to add right-click menu options for quick access to tools. No browsing data is collected or stored.

4. Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated data about site visits (page views, country, browser type). This data cannot be used to identify individual users. You can opt out via Google's opt-out tools.

5. Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show relevant ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

6. Affiliate Links

Some links on our site are affiliate links. Clicking these links may set cookies on your device managed by third-party partners (Namecheap, NordVPN, Canva, Grammarly). We do not have access to these cookies.

7. Cookies

We do not set any first-party cookies. Third-party cookies may be set by Google Analytics, Google AdSense, and affiliate partners.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact
